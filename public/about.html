About the Artist

Calen Gage Buford


Calen G Buford was born in St.Louis, Missouri in the winter of 1996, the youngest of four brothers. His family moved about frequently for the first few years of his life before finally settling down in Park City, Utah. In his youth, he and his brothers enjoyed drawing, but as their passions shifted to other pursuits, Calen remained steadfast in improving his artistic talents.

In his senior year of high school, Calen sold his first piece Above the Rain. Since then he has taken on 20+ commissioned pieces by individuals and companies. While studying Applied Mathematics & Computer Science at Brigham Young University, Calen has continued to stretch his artistic skills by entering new realms of design by creating websites, including this one, from scratch.

In 2015 Calen met a fellow computer science student, Spring Cullen, in a calculus class. On August 17th, 2019 he married Spring in Salt Lake City, Utah. Together they enjoy tackling programming projects, creating art, hiking, and have set goals to learn the morse code alphabet. Calen looks forward to graduating from BYU in 2021, and eventually pursuing an MBA. Regardless of the path his future career takes, Calen plans to always keep art in his life as a form of expression and to uplift others.
